Iced Green Tea by Elizabeth Arden is a Citrus Aromatic fragrance for women. Iced Green Tea was launched in 2001. The nose behind this fragrance is Francis Kurkdjian. Top notes are Mint, Bergamot and Lemon; middle notes are Eucalyptus, Orange and Mandarin Orange; base notes are Mint, Amber and Musk.
