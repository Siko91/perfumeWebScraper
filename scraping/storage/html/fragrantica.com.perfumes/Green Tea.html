Green Tea by Elizabeth Arden is a Citrus Aromatic fragrance for women. Green Tea was launched in 1999. The nose behind this fragrance is Francis Kurkdjian. Top notes are Lemon, Bergamot, Mint, Orange Peel and Rhubarb; middle notes are Jasmine, Oakmoss, Fennel, Musk, Carnation and White Amber; base notes are Green Tea, Jasmine, Oakmoss, Musk, Celery Seeds, Caraway, Cloves and Amber.
