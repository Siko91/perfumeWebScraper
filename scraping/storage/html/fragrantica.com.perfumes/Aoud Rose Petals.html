Perfume rating 3.73 out of 5 with 266 votes
Aoud Rose Petals by Montale is a Floral Woody Musk fragrance for women. Aoud Rose Petals was launched in 2005. The nose behind this fragrance is Pierre Montale. 
This is another magnificent rose fragrance from the Montale oud line, which has several compositions with oud and rose blended together.

Aoud Rose Petals is an unusual and complex composition of flowers and woods, in which voluptuos red rose foregoes deep and noble notes of precious woods (oud, Teak, cedar and Guaiac) with spicy nuances (saffron).
