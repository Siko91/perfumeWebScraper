Insense Ultramarine by Givenchy is a Aromatic Fruity fragrance for men. Insense Ultramarine was launched in 1994. The nose behind this fragrance is Christian Mathieu. Top notes are Black Currant, Watermelon, Bergamot and Galbanum; middle notes are iris, Magnolia, Mint, Carnation, Cardamom, Lily-of-the-Valley and Sage; base notes are Tobacco, Vetiver and Cedar.
