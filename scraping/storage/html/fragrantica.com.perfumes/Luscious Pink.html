Luscious Pink by Mariah Carey is a Floral fragrance for women. Luscious Pink was launched in 2008. Luscious Pink was created by Carlos Benaim and Loc Dong. Top notes are Bellini, Bergamot and Sea Notes; middle notes are Tiare Flower, Peony and Lily-of-the-Valley; base notes are White Musk, Sandalwood and Woody Notes. 
Popular singer Mariah Carey, in cooperation with Elizabeth Arden, welcomes us into her pink world in a summer edition of her second perfume, named Luscious Pink.
 
Luscious Pink was arrived at the market in August 2008. It was created in cooperation with two perfumers, Carlos Benaim and Loc Dong, of IFF. They created the new fragrance containing notes of Sicilian bergamot, accords of ocean breeze and Italian sparkling cocktail Bellini. The heart hides petals of Tiara from Tahiti, lily of the valley and pink peony, while the base notes include accords of bright blond wood, sandalwood, white musk, which is a combination of notes described as a floral melody developing as Mariah\'s voice.
 
The bottle of Luscious Pink is very romantic and contains the same form as its predecessor. The creator is Jean Antretter, senior design director of the house of Elizabeth Arden. The bottle is coloured in pink nuances and yellow and light blue contours this time. Commercial was made by photographer Michael Thompson.
 
The collection that will be presented includes the perfume Luscious Pink in the amount of 30, 50 and 100 ml edp, and 200 ml perfumed body lotion.
 
A beautiful set of miniatures which includes three gracious small flacons with charming butterflies is presented in October 2008. Purple, pink and golden butterflies contain 0.16 fl.oz (5 ml)of fragrant fluid each and are a real treat.
