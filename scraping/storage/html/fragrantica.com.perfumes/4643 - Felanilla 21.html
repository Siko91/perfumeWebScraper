Felanilla 21 by Pierre Guillaume Paris is a Amber Spicy fragrance for women and men. Felanilla 21 was launched in 2008. The nose behind this fragrance is Pierre Guillaume. 
"From the contraction of "Feline" and "Vanilla". A Feline Vanilla with Iris and Safran fur. Neither girly nor food, Felanilla is an adult vanilla.  Precious, generous, racy, Felanilla is a Nectar expressed by three absolutes in majesty in an oriental composition, gently spiced, brightened with a fruity wood under a red amber fire." - a note from the brand.
