Gypsy Water by Byredo is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for women and men. Gypsy Water was launched in 2008. Top notes are Juniper, Lemon, Bergamot and Pepper; middle notes are Pine needles, Incense and Orris Root; base notes are Vanilla, Sandalwood and Amber.
