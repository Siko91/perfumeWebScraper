Perfume rating 3.86 out of 5 with 189 votes
Kn°I by Korloff Paris is a Floral fragrance for women. Kn°I was launched in 2008. The nose behind this fragrance is Daniel Paillasseur. Top notes are Mint, Black Currant and Pineapple; middle notes are Freesia, Lily-of-the-Valley, Sea water, Peony and Rose; base notes are Musk, Cedar and Amber. 
Pure escapism,
the desire for freedom takes the Korloff lady around the world. Over the Andes, she will find happiness. As fresh as a light wind, Bright as a green diamond – the flower of the Andes. A heady perfume combining fresh roses, lily of the valley and white cedar nuances, made for the sensual and mysterious woman of today…

A fresh green composition is as pure as a green diamond, nice leafy nuances are united with a little bit salty tone of water and subtle and airy floral.
