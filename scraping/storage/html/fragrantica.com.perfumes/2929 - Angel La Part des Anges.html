Angel La Part des Anges by Mugler is a Amber fragrance for women. Angel La Part des Anges was launched in 2007. The nose behind this fragrance is Francoise Caron. 
This luxurious edition, heiress of the legendary Angel perfume, was introduced in 2007 carrying “Angel La Part des Anges” name. It is composed of whisky notes, sugary fruit and amber. The edition is limited, available in the luxurious bottle of 10 ml pure perfume.
