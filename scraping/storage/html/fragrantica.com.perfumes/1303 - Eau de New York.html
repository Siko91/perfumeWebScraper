Eau de New York by Bond No 9 is a Citrus Aromatic fragrance for women and men. Eau de New York was launched in 2004. The nose behind this fragrance is Vera Vanore. Top notes are Petitgrain, Bergamot, Grapefruit, Green Notes, Watery Notes and Mandarin Orange; middle notes are Neroli, Lemon Verbena, Basil, Jasmine, Gardenia, Lily and Cyclamen; base notes are Oakmoss, Vetiver, Musk and White Wood.
