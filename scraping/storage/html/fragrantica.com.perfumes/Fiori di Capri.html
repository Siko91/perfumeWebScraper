Fiori di Capri by Carthusia is a Chypre Floral fragrance for women and men. Fiori di Capri was launched in 1948. The nose behind this fragrance is Laura Bosetti Tonatto. Top notes are Ylang-Ylang, Gardenia, Tuberose, White Flowers, Orange Blossom, Orange, Mandarin Orange, Cyclamen, Turkish Rose and Bergamot; middle notes are Carnation, Hyacinth, white honey, Cloves, Wild Strawberry, Incense, iris, Violet Leaf, Pineapple, Plum and Nutmeg; base notes are Oakmoss, White Musk, Sandalwood, Amber, Labdanum, Cedar and Patchouli. 
Unforgettable perfumes. The refinement of the flowery notes of lily-of-the-valley and wild carnation, the fresh chords of amber, sandal, ylang-ylang and oak.
 
The fragrance is available in Parfum 50 ml and Eau de Toilette 50-100 ml.
