Perfume rating 3.95 out of 5 with 455 votes
Carolina by Carolina Herrera is a Floral Woody Musk fragrance for women. Carolina was launched in 2003. Carolina was created by Carlos Benaim and Clement Gavarry. Top notes are Wild Strawberry Leaf, Bitter Orange and Cardamom; middle notes are Forest Fruits, Rose and Pepper; base notes are Cashmere Wood, Musk, Amber and Vanilla. 
Carolina is a tender romance between the tradition and the future; this scent continues the tradition of elegance and femininity of Carolina Herrera’s classic aromas. Wood, amber and light musk create the soft sensuality. Flower petals symbolize femininity; fruit fills it with a fresh juice of energy, while bitter orange and pepper emphasize your individuality. Top notes: bitter orange, wild strawberry leaf and cardamom; middle notes: rose petals, pepper and berries; base notes: woody notes, vanilla, amber and light musk. The fragrance was created by Carlos Benaim and Clement Gavarry in 2003
