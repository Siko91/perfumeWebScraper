Youth-Dew Amber Nude by Estée Lauder is a Amber Vanilla fragrance for women. The nose behind this fragrance is Christophe Laudamiel. Top notes are Cinnamon, Ginger, Tea, Magnolia and Grapefruit; middle notes are Carnation, Ylang-Ylang, Black Rose and Jasmine; base notes are Amber, Sandalwood, Patchouli and Vetiver.
