Perfume rating 3.75 out of 5 with 621 votes
Cabotine Rose by Grès is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Cabotine Rose was launched in 2003. Top notes are Cherry Blossom, Pear, Mandarin Orange and Black Currant; middle notes are Rose, Peony, Mimosa, Pepper, Jasmine, Tuberose, Lily and Orange Blossom; base notes are Frangipani, Musk, Sandalwood and Vetiver.
