Perfume rating 4.43 out of 5 with 801 votes
Insense by Givenchy is a Aromatic Fougere fragrance for men. Insense was launched in 1993. The nose behind this fragrance is Daniel Moliere. Top notes are Aldehydes, Black Currant, Lavender, Basil, Bergamot, Mandarin Orange and Lemon; middle notes are Lily-of-the-Valley, Magnolia and iris; base note is Fir.
