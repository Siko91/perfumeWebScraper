That's Amore! Lei by Gai Mattiolo is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. That's Amore! Lei was launched in 2000. Top notes are Rose, Ginger, Water Notes, Yuzu, Orange and Bergamot; middle notes are Peony, Lily, Magnolia, Violet, Geranium, Jasmine, Freesia and Lilac; base notes are Cedar, Oakmoss, Vanilla and White Musk.
