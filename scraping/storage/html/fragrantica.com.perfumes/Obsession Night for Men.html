Obsession Night for Men by Calvin Klein is a Amber Woody fragrance for men. Obsession Night for Men was launched in 2005. Top notes are Pear, Cardamom, Artemisia and Grapefruit; middle notes are Nutmeg and Musk; base notes are Vanilla, Patchouli and Vetiver.
