Dunhill by Alfred Dunhill is a Amber Woody fragrance for men. Dunhill was launched in 2003. The nose behind this fragrance is Michel Almairac. Top notes are Geranium, Lavender, Lemon and Clary Sage; middle notes are iris, Cedar, Orris Root, Rose, Carnation and Jasmine; base notes are Musk, Tonka Bean, Leather, Sandalwood, Cedar, Vetiver and Oakmoss.
