Perfume rating 3.90 out of 5 with 881 votes
Cat Deluxe At Night by Naomi Campbell is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Cat Deluxe At Night was launched in 2007. Top notes are Black Currant, Tropical fruit, Pear and Pitahaya; middle notes are Peony, Cyclamen and Floral Notes; base notes are Raspberry, Cotton Flower, Amber and Woodsy Notes. 
Flattered by success of her first Cat deluxe, Naomi launches an evening version of her original perfume - Cat deluxe at night, which became more intense. Flowers and exotic fruits replaced a soft peach in the heart of the composition. 

The fragrance opens with green notes and refreshing fruity accords of black currant and pear. The heart notes are peony and cyclamen. The base notes are cotton, amber and woods. 

The perfume was created in 2007.
