Lutece by Dana is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. Lutece was launched in 1984. Top notes are Aldehydes, Brazilian Rosewood, Geranium and Mandarin Orange; middle notes are Peony, Orris Root, Vetiver, Lily-of-the-Valley, Rosemary and Cedar; base notes are Tonka Bean, Vanilla, Heliotrope, Musk and Cinnamon.
