Fire & Ice by Revlon is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. Fire & Ice was launched in 1994. The nose behind this fragrance is Givaudan. Top notes are Tangerine, Orange Blossom and Osmanthus; middle notes are Orchid, Tuberose, Magnolia and Narcissus; base notes are Amber, Incense, Musk and Woody Notes.
