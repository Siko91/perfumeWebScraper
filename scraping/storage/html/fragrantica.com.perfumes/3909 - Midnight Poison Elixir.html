Midnight Poison Elixir by Dior is a Amber Woody fragrance for women. Midnight Poison Elixir was launched in 2008. The nose behind this fragrance is Francois Demachy. 
In cooperation with perfumer Francois Demachy, Dior presents three new versions of their already-existing perfumes. These are the three magical perfumes known as Midnight Poison, Hypnotic Poison, and Pure Poison, which are now presented in the form of an elixir and are available in their well-known bottle shapes and colours in amounts of 30 and 50 ml, with atomizers.
 
The Midnight Poison Elixir perfume will seduce you with new aromas of caramel and vanilla. It was presented in 2008.
