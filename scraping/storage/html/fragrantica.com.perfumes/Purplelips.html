Perfume rating 3.61 out of 5 with 333 votes
Purplelips by Salvador Dali is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. Purplelips was launched in 2006. Purplelips was created by Antoine Lie and Guillaume Flavigny. Top notes are Blueberry and Pomegranate; middle notes are Lilac, Vanilla orchid and Violet; base notes are Amber, Musk and Sandalwood. 
Purple is a sensual, elegant and mysterious colour, and because of that it was chosen for this floral, sensual and oriental fragrance. The heart is made of purple flowers: violet, purple orchid and lilac. Crocus, passion fruit and blueberry are in the top notes. The base is made of amber, sandalwood and musk. Designers are Antoine Lie and Guillaume Flavigny. The perfume was created in 2006.
