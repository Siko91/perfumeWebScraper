Balmain de Balmain by Pierre Balmain is a Chypre Floral fragrance for women. Balmain de Balmain was launched in 1998. Top notes are Galbanum, Currant buds, Bergamot and Pepper; middle notes are iris, Rose, Jasmine and Violet; base notes are Oakmoss, Vetiver, Patchouli and Sandalwood.
