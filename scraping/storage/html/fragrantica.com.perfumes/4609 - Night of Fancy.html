Perfume rating 3.73 out of 5 with 267 votes
Night of Fancy by Anna Sui is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Night of Fancy was launched in 2008. Top notes are Blueberry, Wild Strawberry and Grapefruit Leaf; middle notes are Stephanotis and Jasmine; base notes are Milk, Olibanum and Cashmeran. 
To celebrate 10 years of the house of Anna Sui\'s production of decorative cosmetics, a limited edition of Anna Sui Make Up Color Collection was presented. It includes best sellers and most wanted nuances sold during the previous period, as well as the latest fragrance Night of Sui, which will appear on the market this autumn. What does this night bring and whom will it enchant?

Night of Fancy invites all young women and all those who feel as such, to enjoy and investigate their wildest dreams and fantasies. Feel and experience the mysterious adventure of the successor of Flight of Fancy edition, in new nuances, magical and romantic violet!

The perfume Night of Sui opens with fruity aromas of blueberry with accords of grapefruit leaves and wild strawberries. The heart hides secrets of Stephanotis - jasmine from Madagascar with Sambac jasmine absolute, while the base notes encompass accords of milkshake, olibanum and Cashmeran molecules.

This gracious and elegant edition struts with a top designed in the form of peacock with carved motifs on its tail, and dark floral applications on the glass body of the bottle.
