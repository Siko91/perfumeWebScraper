Dune Pour Homme by Dior is a Woody Floral Musk fragrance for men. Dune Pour Homme was launched in 1997. Dune Pour Homme was created by Olivier Cresp and Jean-Pierre Bethouart. Top notes are Fig Leaf, Cassis, Sage and Basil; middle notes are Fig Wood Bark, Rose and Mignonette; base notes are Sandalwood, Cedar, Tonka Bean and Vanilla.
