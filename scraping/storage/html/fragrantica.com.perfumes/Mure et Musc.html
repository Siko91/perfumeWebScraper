Mure et Musc by L'Artisan Parfumeur is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women and men. Mure et Musc was launched in 1978. The nose behind this fragrance is Jean-Francois Laporte. Top notes are Basil, Amalfi Lemon, Mandarin Orange and Orange; middle notes are Blackberry and Red Berries; base notes are Musk and Oakmoss.
