Byzance by Rochas is a Amber Spicy fragrance for women. Byzance was launched in 1987. Byzance was created by Nicolas Mamounas and Alberto Morillas. Top notes are Aldehydes, Spices, Carnation, Cardamom, Basil, Lemon, Mandarin Orange and Green Notes; middle notes are Tuberose, Ylang-Ylang, Turkish Rose, Jasmine, Orris Root, Anise and Lily-of-the-Valley; base notes are Sandalwood, Heliotrope, Amber, Vanilla, Musk and Cedar.
