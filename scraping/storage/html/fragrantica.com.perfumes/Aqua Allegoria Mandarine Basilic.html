Aqua Allegoria Mandarine Basilic by Guerlain is a Citrus Aromatic fragrance for women. Aqua Allegoria Mandarine Basilic was launched in 2007. The nose behind this fragrance is Marie Salamagne. Top notes are Clementine, Bitter Orange, Orange Blossom, Green Tea and Ivy; middle notes are Mandarin Orange, Basil, Chamomile and Peony; base notes are Sandalwood and Amber.
