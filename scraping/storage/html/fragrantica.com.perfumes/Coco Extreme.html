Coco Extreme by Comptoir Sud Pacifique is a Amber Vanilla fragrance for women and men. Coco Extreme was launched in 2007. Top notes are Coconut and Vanilla Flower; middle notes are Coconut, Milk, Almond and Tonka Bean; base notes are Coconut, Vanille and Sugar.
