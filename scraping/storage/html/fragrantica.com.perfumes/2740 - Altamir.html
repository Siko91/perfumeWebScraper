Altamir by Ted Lapidus is a Amber Woody fragrance for men. Altamir was launched in 2007. Top notes are Neroli, Pineapple Leaf and Bergamot; middle notes are Orange Blossom, Jasmine and Cyclamen; base notes are Tonka Bean, Amber, Musk, Patchouli, Teak Wood and Vetiver.
