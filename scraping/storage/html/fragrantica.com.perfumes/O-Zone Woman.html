O-Zone Woman by Sergio Tacchini is a Floral Green fragrance for women. O-Zone Woman was launched in 2000. Top notes are Lemon, Bergamot and Orange; middle notes are Chamomile, Lavender, Lily-of-the-Valley, Jasmine and Rose; base notes are Green Tea and Cedar.
