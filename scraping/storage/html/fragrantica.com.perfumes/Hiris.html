Perfume rating 4.19 out of 5 with 2,066 votes
Hiris by Hermès is a Floral Green fragrance for women. Hiris was launched in 1999. The nose behind this fragrance is Olivia Giacobetti. Top notes are iris, Coriander, Carnation and Amber; middle notes are iris, Neroli and Rose; base notes are Cedar, Almond Tree, Honey and Vanilla. 
Hiris: from the flower to the fragrance, the modern and refined mindset of a
unique unifloral, all devoted to the splendor of the iris. A perfume of emotion
and subtlety conceived by Olivia de Giacobetti, it expresses its charm with an
infinite delicacy; sometimes floral, sometimes powdery or plant-like, always one
of the olfactory wonders of nature.
