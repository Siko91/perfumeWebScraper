BLV by Bvlgari is a Floral Woody Musk fragrance for women. BLV was launched in 2000. The nose behind this fragrance is Alberto Morillas. Top notes are Ginger and Bergamot; middle notes are Wisteria and Flax; base notes are Musk, Sandalwood, Black locust and Vanilla. 
The cold accords of ginger, pure freshness of gorgeous wisteria and translucent musk are in harmony with the warm depth of sandalwood, feminine sweetness of flax flowers and softness of vanilla and acacia. This perfume was created in 2000 by Alberto Morillas.
