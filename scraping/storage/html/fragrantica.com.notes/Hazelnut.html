Hazelnut is a nut of the hazel tree with edible kernel. The nut is most commonly used raw, roasted or ground into a paste. Hazelnuts are especially tasty in combination with chocolate, while hazelnut oil, pressed from the hazelnuts, can be used in cooking. Hazelnuts are rich in protein and unsaturated fat, thiamine and vitamin B6.
