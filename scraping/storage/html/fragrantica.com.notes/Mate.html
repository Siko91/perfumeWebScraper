Mate is a traditional South American drink that is prepared by steeping dried leaves of yerba mate in hot water. Yerba mate or mate plant is actually a tree and not a herbaceous plant. The mate plant is a shrub of a young tree with evergreen leaves of strongly vegetal, herbal, and grassy flavor. Health benefits of mate drink include its effectiveness in lipid lowering, reduction in risk for cardiovascular diseases, but hot mate drinking is probably carcinogenic to humans.
