Cotton Flower is a soft and fluffy staple fiber of the cotton plant - belonging to the genus Gossypium.The cotton flower forms around the seeds of a plant, and it actually has an important biological function - to aid in seed dispersal. Cotton flower is composed of cellulose, and it is used for centuries to make soft textile.
