Allspice, also known as Jamaica pepper, myrtle pepper, pimenta or newspice, is actually the tiny dried fruit of the Pimenta dioica plant. A native of the Caribbean, Mexico and Central America, the plant is now widely cultivated in warm climates throughout the world and is indispensable in Caribbean jerk seasoning as well as Latin American and Middle Eastern cuisine.
