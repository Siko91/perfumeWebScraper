 12 Parfumeurs Francais Le Roi Chevalier

 
 
male 2018
 
 
 
 
It is not merely a bottle of perfume. It only seems to be one - a pink-blue star from the Angel by Mugler family.
 
 
But actually the moment you spritz the atomizer, a door to a huge old orchard opens, an orchard where apple trees of many different varieties grow. The apples on the trees are yellow, very juicy with their honey like nectar, but also green with their crunchiness and a nearly floral aroma, and glossy red as well, as if especially intended for seduction, and even pink; so pink that you want to kiss the plump firm side as if it were the cheek of a smiling toddler…  This orchard smells like honey and flowers. And apples, certainly apples.
  
 
 
 

 In that orchard stands an old house that is full of that apple spirit; apple jam is sizzling in an open copper pan on the stove, an apple pie is baking in the oven, and some candied apples are already cooling on the window sill.
 
And everywhere around you are baskets of apples, lots and lots of apples. They are neither simple, nor made of gold, yet they are wonderful. They are filled with sudden crushes and unexpected joys, random acts of kindness and unforeseen, spontaneous generosity. Biting into such an apple could make one suddenly realize that an old grudge is forgiven and forgotten, or one can come up with such a bedtime story for oneself that any and all trouble will go away.
 
So what does the new Angel by Mugler eau de toilette smell like?
 It smells like different varieties of apples. And that apple smell is to our surprise so very natural!
 
Before the new Angel, I only came across one even more natural smelling apple fragrance, and that was Lann-Ael by Lostmarch. No, these two perfumes are not similar to one another, they only have a very successful apple note in common
 
In Lann-Ael, the note is more acidic and quiet. Angel has certainly got the sweeter apples, and the fragrance itself is more intense. Those who have ever entered an apple orchard warmed by the sun and filled to the brim with the namesake fruit at the end of August, will surely recognize the aroma. To these apples here, some singing voices of flowers are added. These are not Fall flowers that are somewhat bitter and sad, and not even the peonies promised to us in the press-release, but rose hip flowers that exude their tart-rosy fragrance generously in the air.
  
 
 
 

 The new Angel also smells like apple pastila (a traditional Russian sweet flourless treat made of apple puree, egg whites and sugar that are formed into loaves, baked at low temp, cooled and then сut & served), apple pie, caramel and honey. There is patchouli there too, but it is not as chocolatey as in the classic version of the fragrance, here it is closer to an earthy, autumnal-leafy type of scent. Nevertheless, the delicate floral-fruity fragrance of an apple orchard during the fruits' ripe-and-ready-for-harvest period is the main note in Angel Eau de Toilette.
 
I would not call it a fresher version; the statement that a fragrance is fresh presumes that there are certain harsh citrus, soapy, aquatic notes, or the smell of cleanliness… No, Angel Eau de Toilette is not a fresher version of the classic, but a more tender and delicate one. Basically it is just different. It is similar to the previous version in the same way two half-brothers that have the same mother but come from different fathers can be alike: one is dark-haired, with dark eyebrows and looks like a proud little falcon while the other one is blonde, with gentle features, and no one would ever mistake one for the other.
 
These angelic brothers are much the same way, they cannot be mistaken for one another. One has blue wings, while the half-sibling's plumage is pink-tinged. One smells like chocolate, popcorn, and cotton candy. The other one smells like apples, pastila and flowers. What they have in common are caramel and patchouli and that very intricate olfactory pattern that is somehow repeated throughout all of the Angel by Mugler variations.
 
I admit that some people may not enjoy the new version. But are there really perfumes that are liked by everyone? As for me, the Angel Eau de Toilette became yet another friend from Mugler's Angel army right away. One of those fragrant "troopers" that always make me happy, bring me solace, and adorn me.
 
 
And this apple angel's release has got perfect timing, too - the end of August, beginning of Fall, the very time when there are apples galore everywhere!  It will be most welcome during November's slush and afterwards, on freezing January mornings…
 
Such tender gourmand fragrances always wake up something childlike within one's soul.
 
 
This is not the kind of sweetness that is dripped into the neckline with a crystal dropper as the finishing touch - when you have got scarlet lipstick on the lips, stilettos on your feet, and your mood is to seduce, win, and triumph.
 
Neither is it the kind of sweetness you put on with your fur coat feeling like a tsarina - imperial, warm despite the cold, relaxed and satiated, so you pick a perfume that is also like some intoxicating honey.
 
Angel Eau de Toilette is the tender, poignant kind of sweetness when you read your favorite childhood picture book to your child for the first time - the one your Mom also loved to read to you when you were little.
 
It is the tender kind of sweetness you experience when you realize that you yet again have fallen for an imaginary character from a book or a movie: it will not hurt your existing relationship with your husband in any way, but how precious is that newfound crush which is almost shameful if you are past the age of fifteen (and you definitely are)…
 
 
It is the sweet feeling of picking up a freezing kitten that has lost its voice from all the helpless meowing and you take him home with you. You do realize what you are doing - it is a very irresponsible decision that comes with a lot of responsibility, but it makes you so happy! You feel at ease, because you know how sad and how heavy you would feel if you would leave that kitten meowing in the dark and cold of the streets, when you have both shelter and food for it at home; and you have long decided who to bring home all by yourself. Everybody has their own sweet and tender memories that warm the heart and make you happy every time you suddenly remember one of them. It is a quiet kind of sweetness that brings solace every time. Each time you conjure it in your memory.
 
…My first flacon of Angel by Mugler was a big blue star, an amazing bottle that could only lay flat and not stand on its own: I got it soon after it arrived in the then perfume-deprived Russia. It was a gift. A dream come true.
 
Ever since then, I have never lived without Angel. I have always had that classic star and some of the limited editions that have happened to entice me. Some people loathe the complex chocolate-honey-patchouli sweetness of Angel.
 As for me, it is one of my personal scents of happiness.
 
The concentration of Eau de Toilette, in the flacon reminiscent of a blue comet, was never my favorite: I found it too sweet, despite the bitterish lingonberry smack - and it lacked the thickness and charisma I have always felt present in the Eau De Parfum.
 
However, when I learned that Mugler had plans to change the formula - despite the fact that it was for a concentration that was never to my liking - I became upset. Indeed, in recent years, all changes to fragrances that have already become classics, were only for the worse. I could not believe that I would fall in love with this shining pink new star - which can both stand and lie flat - but most importantly, give comfort and a sense of happiness.
 A different kind of happiness, that's all.
 
 
A more calm, quieter, more personal, more secluded one than that enclosed in the big blue star of Angel Eau De Parfum.
 
I am probably only a bit sad about the flacon, as the previous Eau de Toilette version had a more interesting one.
 
I already know that once my first small flacon of this apple Angel Eau de Toilette is used up, I will buy a new one.
 
Can a grown woman smell like a gourmand apple-caramel-honey fragrance? Well  why the heck not? The perfume is quality made. Despite its intense sweetness, it is refined. Angel Eau de Toilette is quite suitable as the finishing touch to your look. It will hardly offend anyone's sense of smell: it is not aggressive.
 
 
The most important thing for this particular perfume is that it aligns with the inner state of your soul. Angel Eau de Toilette is one of those fragrances that are important for the soul and for the mood. Its exterior manifestation is all sweet and pretty. That is if we smell it close to the skin. The sillage, on the other hand, gives you the air inside a big apple orchard.
  
 
By Elena Prokofeva
