Grapes are fruiting berries of the woody vines that can be eaten raw or used for jams, jellies, juices, wines vinegar, grape seed extracts or raisins. Grapes can be crimson, black, dark blue, yellow, green, orange, and pink, while white grapes are actually green in color. Grape is rich in reservatrol, which is health-beneficial ingredient known to inhibit various ailments such as cancer, heart disease, degenerative nerve disease, viral infections and mechanisms of Alzheimer's disease.
