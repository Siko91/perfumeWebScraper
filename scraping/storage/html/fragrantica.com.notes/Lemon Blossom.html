Lemon blossom is the blooming of the lemon tree. Lemon has a white flower with a yellowish base and five fragrant petals. Lemon trees are among the most cold-sensitive trees and they are blossoming and blooming only with proper care. Lemon tree needs at least six hours of sun exposure a day to be healthy and produce flowers. In colder climates a potted lemon tree should be kept in the house during cold months.
