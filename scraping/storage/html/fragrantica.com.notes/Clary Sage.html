Clary sage, also known as clary or Salvia sclerea is a biennial or perennial herbaceous plant native to Northern Mediterranean. The plant is usually 3 to 4 ft tall. The herb has been used as a medical herb throughout centuries, but it is currently grown exclusively for its essential oil which finds its use in perfumery and as a muscatel flavoring for vermouths, wines, and liqueurs.
